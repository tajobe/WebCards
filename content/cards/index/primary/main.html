WebCards

Welcome to WebCards! This is a simple website using a Card layout to highlight appropriate information.

If you mouse over the card, 3 dots will show at the bottom signifying this card is large enough to be collapsed, and will do so on click. This card is pre-expanded, but they can also automatically be collapsed once reaching a max-height. You can even link to a card on a page to have it be auto-expanded on click.
